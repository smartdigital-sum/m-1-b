Health Tips

Stay Healthy, Stay Happy

Simple tips from our pharmacist to keep you and your family healthy.

💧 Hydration

Drink 8 Glasses of Water Daily

Staying hydrated improves digestion, skin health, and energy levels. Start each morning with a glass of warm water to kickstart your metabolism.

💊 Medicine Safety

Always Complete Your Antibiotic Course

Never stop antibiotics midway even if you feel better. Incomplete courses lead to antibiotic resistance. Consult your pharmacist for guidance.

🌡️ Fever Care

When to Worry About Fever

A fever above 103°F (39.4°C), or any fever in children under 3 months, needs immediate medical attention. Paracetamol helps mild fevers.

🩸 Diabetes

Monitor Blood Sugar Regularly

Diabetic patients should check blood sugar levels daily. We offer Glucometers and test strips at affordable prices. Consistency is key.

👶 Baby Health

Keep Vaccinations Up to Date

Follow the immunisation schedule for your child. Keep a vaccination card safely. Ask us about vitamin drops and supplements for growing babies.

❤️ Heart Health

Check Your Blood Pressure Monthly

High BP often has no symptoms. Visit us for a free BP check. We stock BP monitors and BP medicines at the best prices in Kampur.
